Tribute to Ma'am Rolyn Ann C. Vivares

In honor of a wonderful teacher on Teachers' Day

A Tribute to Ma'am Rolyn

In the halls of wisdom, where dreams take flight,
There stands a beacon, a guiding light.
Ma'am Rolyn, with grace and a heart so true,
You shape our minds, you lead us through.

With every lesson, in every smile,
You go that extra, caring mile.
In Del Rosario, we are one,
Learning, growing, under your sun.

You teach us more than words can say,
You show us kindness, every day.
Through highs and lows, you stand with pride,
Our mentor, friend, and constant guide.

For every question, you have the key,
Unlocking doors for us to see.
With patience, wisdom, and a gentle tone,
You make us feel we're not alone.

Today we honor all you do,
And give our heartfelt thanks to you.
For the knowledge shared, the love you show,
For helping all of us to grow.

Ma'am Rolyn, you're more than just a name,
You're the spark, the joy, the flame.
In Del Rosario, we celebrate,
The teacher who makes our lives so great.

Happy Teachers' Day, with love sincere,
From all your students who hold you dear.
May joy and blessings come your way,
For all the light you bring each day.
